About

I started my career as a Mechanical Engineer in Venezuela, where I learned to read physical systems — how pressure moves, where tolerances fail, why processes break. I brought that operational instinct into data, and it changed how I build dashboards: I don't just visualize numbers, I trace them back to what actually happened on the factory floor.

Today I lead BI at Biffa in the UK, having spent three years before that building global reporting infrastructure for British American Tobacco across multiple regions within a £25B+ organization. My edge is bridging the gap between technical data teams and the commercial leaders who need to act on it.

Fluent in English and Spanish, experienced with international stakeholders across Europe and Latin America, and currently deepening my cloud analytics skills through the Microsoft PL-300 certification.
